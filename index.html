About Us

At Emirate Fresh Market, we are passionate about bringing you the freshest, highest-quality produce, cultivated with love and care

Our Projects

Garden in your Space

This is an urban agricultural initiative still under ideation awaiting proper funding. This project aims to address food insecurity, environmental degradation and youth unemployment across urban settlement and semi urban settlements. It would empower households to grow fresh produce in small spaces. Our goal for this project is to combine food production, environmental restoration and income generation in one simple concept tailored to the Nigeria urban context. The focus on women, youths and under used spaces makes the project inclusive, sustainable and adaptable across cities. This project is not just about growing food, it's about growing resilience, dignity and green cities from the ground up.
